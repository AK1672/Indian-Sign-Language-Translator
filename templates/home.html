Welcome to HandSpeak - Your Gateway to Indian Sign Language (ISL)

Communication is a fundamental human right, and at HandSpeak, we are committed to bridging the gap between spoken and signed languages. Our mission is to create an inclusive world where everyone can connect, regardless of language or hearing ability.

HandSpeak is an innovative platform that transforms hand gestures into words, making Indian Sign Language (ISL) accessible to all. Whether you're a beginner eager to learn ISL, someone looking to communicate more effectively with the Deaf community, or a professional seeking reliable sign language recognition, our project is here for you.

Explore our Learn section to start your journey with ISL, or use the Predict section to see real-time translation of hand signs. We are constantly improving, incorporating new gestures, and adding more features to make ISL learning and communication seamless.
